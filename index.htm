About the Tactile Stories!


Welcome to the Tactile Stories, a store by Tatyana Garkavaya, a craftswoman with over 20 years of experience distinguished by numerous awards.


Mission: I believe in the power of creativity and seek to inspire togetherness, kindness and friendship through creating meaningful and affordable jewellery, toys, and interior decor items. The prices of unique handmade gifts start just at 7$/item!

Beads, natural stones, leather, metal and wool are the primary materials I use for creating gifts for you.

So far, handmade gifts by the Tactile Stories have brightened lives of people from Ukraine, Bulgaria, Germany, Australia, Canada and the USA.

Gifts for any mood and any budget.


Values: I am happy that my creative ideas encourage people to express their feelings in sincere, heartfelt and unique ways.

This behaviour, in turn, creates more occasions for a happy life.

The most fascinating thing about my activity? The joy of giving happy moments whenever people ask me to create original presents for every occasion. This magical power of my handcrafted items has inspired the name of my shop - the Tactile Stories.

I strive to positively impact my customers' lives with Authenticity, Innovation, Passion, and Integrity.
